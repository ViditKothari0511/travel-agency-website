Terms and Conditions

Booking and Reservations

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation. We reserve the right to decline any booking at our discretion.

  • Bookings must be made by individuals 18 years or older
  • All travelers must have valid travel documents
  • Prices are subject to change until full payment is received

Payment Terms

Payment terms vary depending on the type of booking and time until departure:

  • Deposit required at time of booking
  • Full payment required 60 days before departure
  • Late payments may result in cancellation of booking

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation fees apply as follows:

  • 60+ days before departure: Deposit only
  • 30-59 days before departure: 50% of total cost
  • Less than 30 days before departure: 100% of total cost

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that all travelers obtain comprehensive travel insurance. The insurance should cover:

  • Medical expenses
  • Trip cancellation
  • Lost or delayed baggage
  • Personal liability

Changes to Bookings

Changes to bookings are subject to availability and may incur additional charges:

  • Name changes may be subject to airline fees
  • Date changes may result in price differences
  • Changes must be requested in writing

Travel Documents

It is the traveler's responsibility to ensure they have all necessary travel documents:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Required visas
  • Vaccination certificates if required

Liability

We act as an agent for various travel service providers. We are not liable for:

  • Acts or omissions of third-party providers
  • Force majeure events
  • Personal injury or property damage

Complaints

Any complaints should be reported immediately to our local representative or to our office. We will make every effort to resolve the issue promptly.
