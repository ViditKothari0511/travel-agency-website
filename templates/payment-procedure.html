Payment Procedure

Booking and Payment Process

To secure your booking, we require a deposit payment. The remaining balance is due 60 days before your departure date. For bookings made within 60 days of departure, full payment is required at the time of booking.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • Bank Transfers
  • Online Payment Gateways

Payment Schedule

  • Initial Deposit: 20% of total cost
  • Second Payment: 30% of total cost (90 days before departure)
  • Final Payment: Remaining balance (60 days before departure)

Payment Security

All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

If you need to cancel your booking, the following refund policy applies:

  • More than 60 days before departure: Full refund minus deposit
  • 30-60 days before departure: 50% refund
  • Less than 30 days before departure: No refund

Payment Confirmation

You will receive a payment confirmation email immediately after each successful transaction. Please keep this confirmation for your records.
